Engagement Questions

  1. What is the difference between a block and an inline tag? Give examples of each one

    2. Block tags like to take the whole width of the page to themselves. Inline tags do not take up whole width. They share lines unlike block tags.

  2. What is the normal flow of HTML?

    3. Objects are layed out top to bottom.

  3. What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

    4. It allows block tags to share space horizontally. It allows objects to float around it. We need to clear the float when we want to move objects that we don't want to be shared on the same horizontal line as our figure or other object.

  4. What do absolute, fixed and relative do?

    5. They are ways to move things in our page and fix them into the position we want them to.
