My Interests

Interests Hero

I have so many interests. I love music, cuisine, culture, dancing and nature. However, I would like to say I'm extremely interested in sports, especially basketball and rugby.

Why do I love these sports?

Rugby Union

Growing up in Samoa, rugby is the most dominant sport. Kids would play rugby in some field, while the adults were playing volleyball. I had no other choice but to play rugby. The game grew on me. I had a dream to play it professionally, but my physique just couldn't meet the requirement (apparently there are). I still watch rugby on Youtube or television.

Basketball

I got into basketball when my dream of becoming a professional rugby player was dashed. I played along with some friends and slowly got into it more and more. I do have a dream of one day making the NBA, but it feels like a long shot. I love the thrill of the game. The skill, strategy and grit you need to have to win an evenly matched game. I find it very fun and invigorating.
