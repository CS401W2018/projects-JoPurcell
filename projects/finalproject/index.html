Welcome to Samoa Matalasi!

Samoa is a country found in the heart of the Pacific Ocean. It is hard to spot on the map but it is just to the left of the International Date Line (IDL). Prepare to explore what I love about my home land. You will learn about the culture, the different islands, landmarks and more. I have also included my home village and an added bonus, you can create a postcard for yourself or even someone you love!

Culture

Aganu'u (culture) is the foundation of Samoa. We are taught the culture from the time we are able to walk until the day we die. The Samoan culture includes respect and service.

Islands

The islands of Samoa. There are ten, but only FOUR are inhatbited. Each have their own differences in masses, topography and lifestyles.

Landmarks

Explore the various tourist attractions and landmarks that Samoa has in store for you. Discover some of the stories behind some of these landmarks.

Sports

Sports have a great meaning to Samoans. We share a passion for many sports, especially rugby and volleyball. Ta'aloga is a part of every day life.

Vaiola

My homeland. Vaiola is a village in the island of Savaii (see Islands page. Learn about where I come from and where I have lived for the past 11 years!
