Landmarks & Attractions of Samoa

Samoa has a variety of different attractions and landmarks ranging from beaches, mountains and monuments. Read some of these attractions and sites below. If you wish to discover more landmarks and attractions to explore visit the Samoa Travel website

Falealupo

A village on the far side of Savaii, Falealupo is the place to watch the sunset.

Tosua Trench

To-sua means 'giant swimming hole', it is 30 meters deep and you can access the pool by climbing down a long ladder to a platform (as shown in the photo). A cave is inside that leads to the open sea and is recommended for skilled divers.
