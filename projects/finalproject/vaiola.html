My Home: Vaiola

This is my home Vaiola. It is a village inland of Savaii where you can find Church College Vaiola, a school that is under the Church Educational System.

My History with Vaiola

My family moved to Savaii in 2013. I attended Vaiola Primary School and we lived in Fa'ala, Palauli. A year later, we moved to Tapu'ele'ele, the neighbouring village to Vaiola. Then another year later, we finally moved on campus.
