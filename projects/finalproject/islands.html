The Islands of Samoa

There are ten islands in Samoa. However, only 5 are inhabited. The inhabited islands are;

  • Upolu
  • Savaii
  • Apolima
  • Manono
  • Namu'a

Upolu

This island is normally called 'Apia' because of the capital.

Savaii

The bigger island of Samoa normally referred to "Salafai Aulelei" which means beautiful Savaii or the big island of Savaii.
