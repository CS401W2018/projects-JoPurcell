Rugby Hero - Daniel Carter

Growing up watching rugby, I was a huge fan of Daniel Carter. He was the All Blacks fly half (rugby number 10) and I agree with those who say he is the GOAT. He had the skills, the agility and the speed to match. Watching him play as a child was so exhilarating for me. With his famous left boot, Daniel Carter is my hero in rugby.

Achievements

World Rugby Hall of Fame Inductee (2023)
Inducted into the Rugby Hall of Fame, number 162
2x Rugby World Cup Champion
Has won two Rugby World Cups with the New Zealand Rugby team, the All Blacks
3x Super Rugby Champion
He has won 3 Super Rugby titles with the Crusaders

Basketball Hero - Dwayne Wade

I am a Miami Heat fan. I had a debate with who I should put here between Jimmmy Butler and Dwayne Wade. Ultimately, I had to go with "the Flash". My dad speaks highy of Dwayne Wade and how he managed to lead the Heat to a Championship in 2006. I ended up watching some of his highlights, and I ended up becoming a big fan of the Heat AND Wade. Drafted in 2003, he went on to win 3 NBA Championships with the Miami Heat. I believe he is in the top 5 shooting guards of all time.

Season Stats

2003-04 (Miami Heat)
16.2 PPG (points per game)
1.4 SPG (steals per game)
4.5 APG (assists per game)
2004-05 (Miami Heat)
