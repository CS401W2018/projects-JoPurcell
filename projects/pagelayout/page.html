Background

Church College Vaiola, a high school located on the big island of Savaii in Western Samoa. This is one of the three schools in Samoa that is under the educational system of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints (or Church Educational System).

With its illustrious history in sports and academics, Church College Vaiola is considered one of the best high schools in Samoa. The high school has been known to produce star athletes such as Kelvin Masoe and Elisapeta Alofipo, and productive learners such as Claudia Muagututia and Utuaina Peti.
